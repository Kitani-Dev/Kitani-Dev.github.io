  HOT NEW! Take Apart/Put Back Together!
  Our 'Foxy' animatronic can now be transformed into anything your child can imagine. Read more here!

  FAMILY FUN!
  We have tons of activities for your kids to enjoy! Not only do we include a full arcade, but children can also interact with our mascots when off-stage.

  SAFETY FIRST!
  From our food, to our customers, Safety has always been our number one priority. We've had a number of officers from the FSIS approve our food for a straight 3 months.
  On top of food safety, our robotic friends are an engineering marvel. With built in facial recognition, our mascots are sure to keep you and your family safe.

  TOKENS AND PRIZES
  After your child is done playing any arcade game, they will get tickets that they can trade in for prizes and toys. Prizes range from small toys to even plushies!
  How many tickets your child gets is dependent on how well they play. We do NOT accept tickets from past establishments.
